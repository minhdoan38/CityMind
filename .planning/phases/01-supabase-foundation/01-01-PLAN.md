<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Citizen/Next.js to FastAPI | Untrusted report fields and evidence cross the public ingest boundary. |
| Officer/Next.js to FastAPI | A bearer token crosses into protected officer endpoints. |
| FastAPI to Supabase | Caller JWT clients remain RLS-scoped; privileged clients bypass RLS and are server-only. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-01-01 | Spoofing | `backend/app/security.py` | high | mitigate | Verify JWKS signature, issuer, audience, expiry, and officer/admin claim; fail closed. |
| T-01-02 | Elevation of Privilege | Supabase clients/RLS | critical | mitigate | Separate caller-scoped and service-role clients; enforce role policies and never expose the privileged key. |
| T-01-03 | Information Disclosure | reports/evidence/access tokens | high | mitigate | Deny anonymous reads, keep bucket private, and constrain access-token persistence to hashes. |
| T-01-04 | Tampering | status history | high | mitigate | Officer/admin INSERT-only RLS policy with explicit UPDATE/DELETE denial tests. |
| T-01-05 | Denial of Service | public analyze | medium | mitigate | Preserve bounded image validation and rate limiting before AI/storage calls. |
| T-01-SC | Tampering | npm/pip installs | high | mitigate | Blocking human checkpoint for all seven SUS packages, exact pins, lockfile review, `npm ls`, and `npm audit --omit=dev`. |
</threat_model>
